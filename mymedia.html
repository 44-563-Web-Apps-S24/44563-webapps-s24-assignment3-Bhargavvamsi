Interesting Fact about My Favorite Book

"Gulliver's Travels" was anonymously published in 1726, with Jonathan Swift later confirmed as the author. The anonymity heightened the impact of Swift's satire, leaving readers to speculate on the true identity of the author.

The OR command in SQL displays a record if any of the conditions separated by OR is TRUE.

        SELECT column1, column2, ...
        FROM table_name
        WHERE condition1 OR condition2 OR condition3 ...;
